An Educational Bathtime Companion

Bathtime can be a magical time to learn and play for kids - given the right toy!

Floobes is a bath toy that challenges kids to use simple tools like pipes to solve big problems, all while having fun in the tub.





BUILD

With Floobes, kids can find and assemble pipe pieces together to solve puzzles of water crisis and bring water from a tap to the gameboard where the town of Floobie is in dire need of hydration.



LEARN

Understanding the basis of flow analysis & how those concepts apply to the problem of drought, as water flows through the pipe assemblies to the goal compartments of the gameboard, is encouraged in a visual, hands-on experience.

Kids get to exercise cognitive and analytical skills while puzzling over pipes of a smorgasbord of configurations.




COLLABORATE

Floobes is for everybody! Kids can gather up friends and create crazy assemblies together in a backyard pool, garden or secret clubhouse.

The possibilities are endless! Get a kit and let your kids start making a universe of pipes to save the day!
